   What exactly are Payday Loans? Payday loans, also referred to as Payday advances, are short term cash loans given against your next paycheque. Payday loans can be for amounts of up to £750 at a time which will be due for repayment on your next payday – repayment of payday loans can be for periods of up to 30 days at a time – sometimes longer should you decide to extend your repayment loan term.
   What can Payday Loans be used for? The cash from a payday loan can be used for just about anything you need it for – example: to settle a few outstanding bills, car repair, a plumbing emergency or just to enjoy a fantastic weekend away, it’s really up to you!
   How would one Qualify for Payday Loans?
  • You must have a regular form of income of at least £750 per month, which gets paid into an active bank account with a debit card,
  • Be over the age of 18,
  • Reside in England, Scotland or Wales and be entitled to work in the UK.
   How much can I borrow when applying for a Payday Loan? You can borrow any amount from £80 to £750 at one time. The cash amount for which you will qualify (your credit limit) will be based on your net monthly pay.
   How much do Payday Loans cost? You will repay £100 for every £80 you borrow. There are absolutely no administration fees. Typical Example: Loan – £80; Interest – £20; Total Repayable – £100; Term: 30 days; Typical APR – 1355%.

   Can I get an extension on my payday loan? Yes, you can extend part, or all, of your cash loan easily until the following payday by simply paying the interest-only on the original cash loan amount. This is also known as deferring your loan repayment.
