How Payday Loans Work

Payday loans can give you access to quick, effortless cash now, just when you need it most.

What do I need to Qualify?

  • Reside in England, Scotland or Wales

That’s it! There’s no need to fax any documents or cheques. The whole payday loan lending process takes under 6 minutes from start to finish to complete – this includes signing your loan agreement!

Please note that all information is sent via secure 128-bit SSL connection for your security!

* Occasionally we will be unable to confirm all details automatically. Should this be the case we will ask you to fax in simple documentation for us to be able to approve your loan.
