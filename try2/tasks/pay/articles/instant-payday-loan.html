An Instant Payday Loan – Some Quick Facts

Instant payday loans are loans that are taken on a short-term basis by people who face a cash crisis between paydays. It is not hard to get a payday loan since the process is quite simple.

All the applicant needs is a job that pays a regular salary and a current banking account to be eligible for a same day cash loan.

Applying For an Instant Payday Loan

Also referred to as a no credit check payday loan, an instant payday loan involves an easy application procedure, which results in the loan amount being deposited into the borrower’s bank account.

These days, this has been made even more easy thanks to online transactions. The advantage is that the payday loan can be applied at any time from any computer.

These websites feature a convenient calculator that works out a repayment plan for the applicant. Usually the money borrowed is due on the next payday. However, some lenders are agreeable when it comes to extending the date of repayment. This would attract extra charges from the borrower.

Depending on the prevailing payday loan laws and the individuals’ monthly income, there’s a specific amount that can be borrowed as an instant payday loan.

Interest Rates And Fees

With these types of loans the cost to the borrower is the transaction fees and interest. The lender does not go into an investigation of the applicants credit history.

Thus, even if someone has a case of bad credit, they too can apply for a payday loan, though the interest rates would be higher. There are lenders whose terms will call for a flat fee per day for the duration of the loan instead of a rate of interest.

The lenders advertise an APR or annual percentage rate based on which the person seeking the instant payday loan can compare different lenders to see who offers the best rates for his particular needs.

Instant Payday Loans – 100% Online

This can be done from the comfort of any personal computer since many online lenders will offer the facility to apply online, attracting customers with payday loans at low interest rates. For those borrowing for the first time, some lenders will even waive off the initial service fee.

Compared to other loans, these loans are much easier to get, more so because, as implied by the name, they can be obtained instantly. This is especially convenient for those in need of urgent cash. The best part is, an instant payday loan do not have a bearing on the borrower’s credit record when the repayments are made within the stipulated date.
