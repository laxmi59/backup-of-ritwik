When to use a Payday Loan and When to Use a Bank Loan

When you need to purchase something for which you do not have ready cash, you will need to obtain a loan. There are two different types of loans. They are bank loans and payday loan. In which instances should you choose a bank loan or a payday loan?

The answer depends on what you wish to purchase.

Before determining which type of loan is best for which purchases, it is important to understand the differences between payday loans and bank loans. There are actually some similarities between payday loans, or cash advances, and bank loans. With both a payday loan and a bank loan, you must complete an application and meet qualification requirements. Payday loans are different from bank loans. In most instances, a bank loan will take at least a few days in order to process. Conversely, a payday loan can be secured within around a day.

With a payday loan, your credit history is an important part of being able to qualify for and receive the loan. With a payday loan, you are only required to have a job and a checking account in order to be approved.

Bank loans are typically for a long period of time while payday loans are generally short term.

Under which circumstances are these two different types of loans used? Bank loans are generally used when a larger investment is needed.

This is often the case when purchasing real estate, a vehicle or a business investment. These types of loans generally begin at around £1,000.

Payday loans are typically used in a different manner. Suppose you are behind on your rent. If you are not able to pay at the end of the week, you will be evicted. The problem is that your payday does not arrive until the following week. A payday loan can easily solve this problem by providing you with a cash advance until your next payday.

A payday loan can also be beneficial if your automobile requires repairs and you need to have it repaired so that you can travel to work. Payday loans provide you with the ability to obtain the money you need quickly.

When you need money quickly, payday loans the same day with no credit check provide the ideal solution. When you need a larger amount of money, over £1,000, consider a bank loan.
