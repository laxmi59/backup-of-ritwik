More about Fast UK Payday Loans

Getting A UK Payday Loan

Fast UK payday loans UK also known as a UK cash advances are convenient short-term loans to help you manage your financial requirements until you get your next paycheque. Lenders who offer no credit check, UK payday loans do not expect you to offer any collateral against the loan. These temporary payday loans are generally available for a maximum duration of 31 days and you are expected to arrange to repay them on or before your next salary date.

How To Apply And Get Approved For A UK Fast Payday Loan

With UK payday loans, no credit checks are done. Because of the very nature of these loans, lenders expect the applicants to meet some eligibility criteria as follows. The applicant must;

  • Be a UK resident
  • Be at least 18 years of age
  • Be regularly employed for at least six months
  • Have proof of residence
  • Hold a bank account for at least the last three months

Once the UK cash advance loan is approved, the lender transfers the money into the applicant’s bank account within a few hours as promised. This money can be used for taking care of overdue bills, vehicle repairs, medical emergencies, health expenses, a much deserved holiday or anything else you can think of. With these UK payday loans no credit check is necessary for the applicant to be approved for the loan.

How Much Can You Borrow On A Payday Loan?

With a UK payday advance loan, you could borrow £80 – £1000, depending on your monthly income. This money must be repaid on your next payday. The whole process can be done online by arranging for your account to be debited automatically on the loans repayment due date. Some lenders will let you extend your loan if you are unable to repay it at the mutually agreed time, but this is generally avoidable as far as possible since it can work out to be a very expensive event.

Even though fast UK payday loan lenders do not check your credit history, they will still need assurance that you will have enough money to pay back the loan on the due date.
