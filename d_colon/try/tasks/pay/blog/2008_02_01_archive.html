With the world coming into a more computer savvy stage online pay day loans have been able to adapt with this modern day trend. They are there for you when you need in extreme financial emergencies or when you just need that little extra help until your next pay day.

Bad Credit Payday Loans

You’re awarded a bad credit history when you consistently fail to repay a loan on time or make continuous defaults while repaying a loan. Whatever the reason might be, if you fail to repay a debt efficiently, it will reflect as a bad mark on your credit history. The problem with a bad credit history is that you won’t be able obtain other financial products at a decent interest rate.

Payday loans may be used by those who need some emergency cash. Since pay day loans do not require borrowers to provide any collateral as security, consumers can acquire a loan almost immediately. As the name suggests, such loans are provided to help borrowers manage their expenses until their next pay date. This loan is best suited for those people who are running a tight budget, or for those who may need some extra cash to tide over a pressing emergency.

Since the credit history of a borrower is not usually assessed for a payday loan, all kinds of customers with a bad credit history may apply for a pay day loan. Borrowers can apply for amounts of between £80 and £1000 pounds in some cases. The loans are available for a period of up to 31 days and the borrower is expected to return the money within the said time frame. If the borrower needs to extend the loan, he or she will need to apply for an extension and pay interest for the days that they extended the loan for. The disadvantage with such a loan is that lenders will charge a high interest rate because the loan is provided without the backing of collateral. But these interest rates are much lower than acquiring an overdraft on your bank account or even the penalty charges on a credit card.

Payday loans are extremely good to use in an emergency when you just can’t wait that long to acquire some much needed cash. Therefore payday loans should not really be used for anything other than to help you out of an immediate pressing financial fix. While it is a very convenient way to obtain money quickly for your short term needs, consumers who obtain a payday loan without having an idea of how to repay it will most likely be running a financial risk.

Prepaid Credit Cards – Convenience at your fingertips!

The newest fad these days are pre-paid credit cards. Why? Simply because they offer everyday consumers with an opportunity to own a zero risk credit card to suit their needs and lifestyle.

Perhaps you’re looking at getting a credit card but are unsure if you will qualify. Perhaps you've had some past credit problems, a poor credit history, or have no guaranteed income with which to apply? Alternatively, maybe you want the security of having a credit card in the case of an unexpected emergency, but without the risk of overspending or being overwhelmed with interest charges?

Well now there's an answer to suit your needs! Prepaid credit cards are a great way to get your hands on a credit card without any of the associated risks involved. These cards work on the same principle as a standard credit card but with one major difference, there’s no line of credit. Prepaid credit cards have to first be loaded with cash before you can use them for spending, which eliminates the possibility of getting into a financial fix. Simply top up your card and off you go! Most of the major financial institutions offer these types of cards to customers who might otherwise find it difficult to get a credit card elsewhere. There's no need for a credit check and there's no interest charges either!

However some providers do charge a small fee, sometimes as a once off card issuing charge, to open your account. Prepaid credit cards are accepted everywhere just like ordinary cards which makes them very useful to have indeed.

4 Ways to use payday loans responsibly

Everyone has been in a financial fix before and a lifesaver for the majority of consumers is a payday loan. Some upper class and even middle class people tend to see these loans as beneath them, but nothing could be further from the truth. The middle class are often the ones that make the most use of payday loans. When you are faced with some unexpected emergency or expenses, they can really come in handy.

Payday loans have a somewhat bad reputation mainly because a great number of consumers use them irresponsibly.

1) The best way to judge whether you need a payday loan is to ask yourself whether you are able to get by without one. Never use a payday loan to purchase something that you can live without, unless it is somehow crucial to how you go about making a living. Payday loans are always best used for emergencies only or as a last financial resort. If you have money you were saving for something else, rather use that instead. You can always start saving again at a later stage.

2) Never borrow more money than you know you can easily pay back when it’s due. Most pay day loans are normally due within 30 days. Make sure you know for sure you will have the money to repay the loan with.

3) Understand that you do not get something for nothing. All payday loans do accrued interest. Make sure to factor this in when you consider getting a pay day loan.

4) Borrow only what you need at the time. You may be tempted to borrow up to your limit, but consider the fees you will have to repay later.
